## Standard operators
Same as **JAVA*

## Kotlin specific operators
* **Unary plus:** `+x`
* **Unary minus:** `-x`
* `in` & `!in` (`a in b` => `b.contains(a)`, `	a !in b` => `!b.contains(a)`)
* `shl` - Signed shift left
* `shr` - Signed shift right
* `ushr` - Unsigned shift right
* `and` - Bitwise and
* `or` - Bitwise or
* `xor` - Bitwise xor
* `inv` - Bitwise inversion
